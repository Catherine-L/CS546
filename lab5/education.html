Middle School

The middle school I attended is named Lida Middle School in Suzhou. I was very bad at any classes required logical thinking, especially Maths. I used to read a lot and favorite class was History. At this moment, there is not anything detailed I could remember about the life in middle school.

High School

I went to Soochow Academy at my hometown. My favorite class was Global History, and I still hated Maths the most just like the past.

The school has some great international program, when I was a freshman, some exchange students from high school in United States arrived. It is probably my first time interact with foreigh students.
